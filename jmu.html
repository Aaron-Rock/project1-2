James Madison University

James Madison University was founded in 1908 and is a public unviersity located in the center of Harrisonburg. JMU is home to 19,550 undergraduate students and 1,946 graduate students. There is 8 different undergraduate degree programs that encompass the 45 different majors offered at the university. There is 76 doctoral, master's and graduate programs available. James Madison also offers several professional and contining education programs for people looking for a more untraditional approach to high education.
